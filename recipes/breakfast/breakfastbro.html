breakfast burrito

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Black beans
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • salsa

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside. Drain and rinse the black beans. Chop the tomatoes and shred the cheese. Heat a skillet over medium hea
  2. Step 2:Add a bit of oil to the skillet and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble them until cooked through. Add the black beans and chopped tomatoes to the eggs. Cook for a few more minutes until heated through. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the eggs and beans. Let it melt.
